# Sonance EQ — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-20_

Sonance EQ processes audio **entirely on your device**. The app applies your equalizer to your Mac's
audio output in real time.

## What we collect
**Nothing.** Sonance EQ has no analytics, no accounts, and no servers of its own. Your audio is never
recorded, stored, or transmitted off your device unless **you** explicitly start a recording, which
writes only to a file you choose.

## Permissions
- **Audio capture** — required to read your Mac's audio so the equalizer can process it. Audio stays on
  your device.

## Purchases
The optional one-time **Pro** unlock is processed by Apple and RevenueCat. Apple/RevenueCat handle the
transaction per their own privacy policies; Sonance EQ receives only an anonymous entitlement status.
